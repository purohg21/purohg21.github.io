My Food Obsession!

Sure; food is a basic need and we wouldn’t be able to survive without it. But, I don’t eat only when I’m hungry.

I could be craving a rich chocolate cake after eating an extremely heavy meal and eat half of the whole cake, no problem. It’s easy to unleash my Furious Pete when I’m really hungry and the food is really good. But sometimes, there are days when I just don’t know what to eat, but there are dishes for every mood, cuisines for every dinner.
